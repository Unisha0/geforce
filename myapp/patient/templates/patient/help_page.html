Help & Support

How to Book an Ambulance

Booking an ambulance for emergency services is easy! Just follow these steps:

  1. Login to your account and visit the Ambulance section from the sidebar.
  2. Enter the necessary details like the type of emergency and your location.
  3. Select the hospital or choose your desired destination.
  4. Click Book Ambulance to confirm your request.
  5. You will receive a confirmation, and an ambulance will be dispatched to your location.

Hospital Information

For hospital-related information, follow these steps:

  • Visit the Hospital section from the sidebar.
  • You will find a list of available hospitals that are affiliated with our service.
  • Click on the hospital to view its details such as contact info and services offered.

Account Settings

To manage your account settings, follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the Account Setting section in the sidebar.
  • Here, you can update your personal details, change your password, and configure notifications.
